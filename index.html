What’s Included — Both Options
Full RMM — monitoring, patching, helpdesk 8×5⌄
Your computers, servers, and devices are proactively monitored. Software is kept up to date automatically, and your team has a real help desk to call when something goes wrong — so IT problems don’t become lost billable hours.
Managed EDR, SIEM, and DNS protection⌄
Three layers of defense working together: EDR stops threats on individual devices, SIEM analyzes activity across your entire network to catch unusual patterns, and DNS protection blocks malicious websites before anyone on your team can reach them.
Security awareness training program⌄
Most breaches start with a single employee clicking the wrong link. Your team receives regular, short training modules and simulated phishing tests so they can recognize and report threats before they become incidents.
Incident response planning + coordinated response⌄
If something does go wrong, you won’t be scrambling. You’ll have a documented, rehearsed plan and a SecureNA team that knows your environment and coordinates the response — minimizing downtime, financial impact, and reputational exposure.
Automated backup — workstations and server⌄
Your data is automatically backed up on a regular schedule and stored securely off-site. In the event of ransomware, hardware failure, or accidental deletion, your business can be restored quickly.
Dedicated onboarding and migration support⌄
A SecureNA engineer manages the entire transition from your current setup — deploying tools, configuring your environment, and running a full kickoff session with your team.

Frequently Asked Questions
What if we already have an IT provider or vendor?⌄
SecureNA works alongside existing vendors or manages a clean transition away from them. We handle the coordination, migration, and handoff so your team doesn’t have to. Most clients are fully transitioned without a single day of service interruption.
How long does onboarding actually take?⌄
Your kickoff call is scheduled within 5 business days of signing. Full environment setup and tool deployment typically completes within 2–3 weeks depending on scope. Your team has a named point of contact throughout.
What does the rate escalation mean for our budget?⌄
The rate increases are pre-set and automatic — $140 in Year 1, $147.50 in Year 2, $155 in Year 3. No surprise increases and no renegotiations. Additional fees may apply for out-of-scope work. You can plan your core IT budget for the full 36 months with confidence.
What happens if we want to add locations during the term?⌄
Option A is specifically structured for this. Your per-user rate is locked — any additional users or sites come on at the same $140–$155 per user depending on which year you’re in. No new negotiations, no higher rates as you grow.
What if something goes wrong after we sign?⌄
You’ll have a named SecureNA point of contact, not a support ticket queue. If there’s ever a security incident, your incident response plan activates immediately with our team coordinating the response. Proactive monitoring means threats are identified early.
How is SecureNA different from our last provider?⌄
SecureNA leads with security, not just IT support. We assessed your environment in March 2025, identified exactly where the gaps were, and built this program specifically around those findings. Most managed IT providers react to problems. We’re structured to prevent them.
